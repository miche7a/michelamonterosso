I'm a Senior UX Designer based in Stockholm with 10+ years of experience across branding, interaction design, and product strategy. I started as a graphic designer in Tokyo, retrained through Hyper Island, and have been leading UX work since.

My practice is research-led and human-centred — I care about the problem behind the problem, ask a lot of WHYs, and bring a cross-disciplinary perspective shaped by working across cultures, industries, and time zones.

Currently open to new opportunities.

Strengths
Chaos to structure Thrives on complexity — tangled flows, edge cases, competing constraints — and maps them into something plannable and clear.
People to outcomes Brings designers, developers, and stakeholders together — facilitates, synthesises, and turns it into concrete features and milestones.
Reliable by default Consistently fast, consistently high quality, and always straight about risks and limits. Teams know what they're getting.
Deep domain empathy Gets genuinely immersed in any field — editorial, nautical, construction — and designs from the inside out.
Weaknesses
Design system libraries Building complex component libraries with deep variants and tokens — I understand the logic, I've worked with brand systems for years, but it's not the kind of execution work I'm looking for.
Staying creative under pressure When stress spikes, I default to pragmatism. Learning to protect creative space even in crunch.
Deadline anxiety Sustained time pressure takes a toll. I manage it by planning thoroughly upfront — but it's still something I'm working on.
