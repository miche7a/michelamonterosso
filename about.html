About

I'm UX Lead / Senior Product Designer based in Stockholm with 15 years of experience across branding, interaction design, and product strategy. I started as a visual designer, retrained during my career, and have been leading UX work since.
I bring a multi-disciplinary perspective shaped by working across cultures and industries. I specialise in complex digital products, turning ambiguity into clear UX direction.

Experience highlights

  • Leading UX direction for complex digital products across multiple industries
  • Turning ambiguous problem spaces into structured opportunities with a clear design direction
  • Facilitating stakeholder workshops and presentations to create alignment and support decision-making
  • Delivering UX work across discovery, concept, and delivery phases
  • Bridging user needs, business priorities, and technical constraints across teams
  • Contributing to developing the design competence area
  • Supporting career development through coaching, feedback, and development talks
