I'm a Senior UX Designer based in Stockholm with 10+ years of experience across branding, interaction design, and product strategy. I started as a graphic designer in Tokyo, retrained through Hyper Island, and have been leading UX work since.

My practice is research-led and human-centred — I care about the problem behind the problem, ask a lot of WHYs, and bring a cross-disciplinary perspective shaped by working across cultures, industries, and time zones.

Currently open to new opportunities.

Strengths
Chaos to structureI thrive when things are complex. Complex flows, edge cases, competing constraints are some of my favourite challenges.
People to outcomesI'm good at bringing people together across disciplines, facilitating the conversation, and making sure it leads somewhere concrete.
Deep domain empathyI immerse myself fully in whatever field I'm designing for. Whether it's editorial, logistics, automotive, or healthcare, I learn the language and design from the inside.
Honest notes
Design system librariesI understand how component libraries work as I spent years building brand systems, but deep token architecture isn't the kind of work that excites me.
Staying creative under pressureWhen stress spikes I default to pragmatism. I'm working on protecting creative space even when things get tight.
Craft over coordinationI tend to lose my spark when I spend most of my time away from my hands-on UX crafting. I need to stay close to design, not just in the product process.
